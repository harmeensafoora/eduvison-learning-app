Features

A system that watches gently,
then adapts decisively.

I don't ask you your learning style. I infer it from how you move: what you reread, where you switch modality, and how your recall scores change.

Hover the cards. Everything should feel alive, not flashy.
Personalization

Learns your style

If you do better with visuals, I lead with the graph. If you do better with analogies, I shift to story-driven explanations. If you learn by doing, I surface practice first.

Memory

Remembers where you struggle

When a concept stays below 50% twice, I flag it as close and bring it back with a gentler framing. No judgment. Just signal and repeat.

Guidance

Tells you what comes next

After each moment, I rank the next step: revisit a near-miss, continue an unlocked node, or strengthen weak areas before finishing.

Assessment

Adaptive quizzes

I adjust difficulty based on recent recall: medium to hard when you're consistently strong, and down to easy when you're not.

Cognitive

Cognitive load checks

If you revisit too much or time-on-chunk spikes, I surface a small prompt to slow down and reset.

Mastery

Mastery visualization

I color concepts by where you stand: mastered, in progress, struggling, or locked until prerequisites are met.
